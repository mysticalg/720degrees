🛹 Skate 720: Park Circuit

Original HTML5 tribute with isometric flow, tickets, shops, and four courses.

HUD

  • 🎫Tickets: 0
  • ⭐Score: 0
  • ⛸️Course: A
  • ⏱️Time: 0
  • 🎚️Difficulty: Normal

Controls

Move: Arrow Keys / WASD

Ollie: Space

Air Spin: Space (while airborne)

Pause: P

Menu Back: Esc

💡 Tooltips are enabled throughout the interface. Hover items in menus for details.
